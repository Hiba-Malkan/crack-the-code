how to play

the reel doesn't stop turning til you make a guess. press space when the pointer lands on the digit you want to choose.

only an exact digit in the exact spot shows green. everything else, right digit, wrong spot or just wrong shows gray.

6 guesses are the maximum number of attempts you get to secure a win. past that, try as long as you want.
